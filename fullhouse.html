Visit Miracle Shops

Miracle Mile Shops, situated on the Las Vegas Strip near Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino, is a bustling shopping mall with over 200 stores and eateries, offering a lively and diverse retail experience. Known for its vibrant atmosphere, it's a popular destination for both shopping enthusiasts and tourists in Las Vegas.

Try out Capos Las Vegas

Capo's Italian Steakhouse & Speakeasy is a luxurious mob-style restaurant that has a speakeasy theme, live nightly entertainment, and incredible food will truly offer an evening that you won't find anywhere else in Las Vegas.

Big Bus Las Vegas

Admire the glittering lights of the Strip on this evening double-decker bus tour. Along the three-hour drive, you'll pass well-known casinos like the MGM Grand and the Bellagio before hopping off to enjoy the free light shows on Fremont Street

Stay at Sahara Las Vegas

The Sahara Las Vegas Hotel, located on the iconic Las Vegas Strip, offers a blend of modern luxury and classic Vegas charm. With stylish accommodations, diverse dining options, and a vibrant casino, it provides a dynamic and memorable experience for guests

Get Luky at Bellagio

The Bellagio Casino, situated on the Las Vegas Strip, is a luxury resort known for its iconic dancing fountains and upscale amenities. With elegant accommodations and a sophisticated casino, it remains a premier destination for those seeking a lavish and entertaining experience in Las Vegas
