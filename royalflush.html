Our team of experts has scoured a collection of handpicked activities and insider tips to ensure your journey is extraordinary.

Visit Hakkasan Nightclub

Hakkasan Nightclub in Las Vegas, located at the MGM Grand Hotel & Casino, is a world-renowned nightlife destination famous for hosting top-tier DJs and performers. With its opulent design, cutting-edge sound systems, and energetic atmosphere, Hakkasan offers a premier nightlife experience in the heart of the Las Vegas entertainment scene.

Try out The Shag Room

The Shag Room is an intimate and sexy space designed to host a variety of social events and can also be reserved for private affairs. The perfect place for cocktails and to really let your hair down. With all the amenities, including a curated bar, state-of-the-art audiovisual, and a fun groovy vibe, you won’t want to be anywhere else.

Take a Helicopter Tour

See the bright lights of the Las Vegas Strip on this whirlwind helicopter flight. Soar over this famous stretch of Las Vegas Boulevard for a bird's-eye view of Sin City’s downtown skyline and beyond, including the Bellagio, the Mirage, and the Fremont Street Experience.

Stay at Paris Las Vegas

Paris Las Vegas is known for its stunning half-scale replica of the Eiffel Tower, which offers breathtaking views of the Las Vegas Strip. The resort also features a replica of the Montgolfier Balloon, the world's largest helium balloon, which takes guests on a ride with panoramic views of the city.

Get Lucky at Wynn

Wynn Las Vegas is a luxury resort and casino located on the Las Vegas Strip, known for its opulent design, world-class dining, and entertainment options. It features an 18-hole golf course, an artificial mountain with a 45-foot waterfall, and a beautiful atrium filled with thousands of hand-picked flowers.
